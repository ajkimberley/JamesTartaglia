Philosopher and Jazz Musician

Welcome to my website. I'm a British philosopher working at the Slovak Academy of Sciences, who tries to write original philosophy books that might interest both the general public and professionals. I'm also a jazz musician who makes Jazz-Philosophy Fusion. My main books are published by Bloomsbury: Philosophy in a Meaningless Life (2016) and Philosophy in a Technological World: GODS AND TITANS (2020). I am a Director of the DFK Community Wellbeing Music & Arts Hub. I hope you find something of interest.

Thanks for visiting!
